Q: How does your AI work?
A: Our AI uses five advanced techniques: Collaborative Filtering (recommendations based on similar customers), Content-Based Filtering (suggestions based on ingredients/flavors you like), Hybrid Recommendations (combining multiple approaches), Clustering (grouping similar cakes), and Clean Algorithm (unbiased, diverse suggestions).

Q: What is collaborative filtering?
A: Collaborative filtering analyzes customers with similar tastes to yours. If customers like you loved Belgian Choco Bliss and Galaxy Dream, you'll probably love them too! It's like having thousands of cake-loving friends making recommendations.

Q: What is content-based filtering?
A: Content-based filtering examines the ingredients, flavors, and characteristics of cakes you've liked before, then suggests similar options. Love chocolate? It'll recommend our chocolate family: Belgian Choco Bliss, Chocolate Overload, Mini Choco Treat.

Q: What are hybrid recommendations?
A: Hybrid recommendations combine collaborative filtering, content-based filtering, and other AI techniques to give you the most accurate suggestions possible. It's like having multiple cake experts working together for you.

Q: How does clustering work?
A: Clustering groups similar cakes together, helping you discover new favorites. If you love rich chocolate cakes, clustering shows you our entire chocolate family, plus suggests similar options you might not have considered.

Q: What is the clean algorithm?
A: Our clean algorithm removes outliers and ensures diverse recommendations. It prevents bias, ensures you see variety, and removes fake or manipulated data to give you genuinely helpful suggestions.
