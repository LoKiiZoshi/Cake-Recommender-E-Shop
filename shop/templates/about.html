Our Story

Smart Cake Shop was born from a passion for baking and a vision to revolutionize how people discover their perfect cake.

Founded in 2020, we started as a small bakery with a big dream: to use artificial intelligence to understand customer preferences and recommend the perfect cake for every occasion. Our journey began when our founder, a passionate baker and tech enthusiast, realized that choosing the right cake could be overwhelming with so many options available.

Today, we're proud to serve over 1000 happy customers with our unique blend of traditional baking techniques and modern AI technology. Our recommendation system learns from customer preferences, seasonal trends, and occasion-specific requirements to suggest the perfect cake every time.
